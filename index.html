How to Play

▾

The Goal

First team to 21 wins. Offense creates a quality shot before the 24-click clock dies; defense contests and drains the clock.

Your Turn

  1. Offense: tap one of your gold pieces, then tap an adjacent zone to move it (costs 1 click). Or tap Shoot (free, ends the possession).
  2. Defense: tap a blue piece, tap an adjacent zone to move (1 click), then Done defending.

The 24-Click Clock

Both teams share one clock. Every move spends 1 click. Run it to 0 with no shot = shot-clock violation, ball turns over.

Shot Quality → Dice

Coverage at the moment you shoot decides how many dice you roll:
CoverageDice
Wide open (no defender within 1 zone)3
Open (defender 1 zone away)2
Contested (defender same zone)1
Clock at 6 or below? Subtract 1 die (pressure), min 1.

Making the Shot

Each zone has a threshold. Roll your dice — if any die meets or beats the threshold, it's a bucket (+2). Lower threshold = better shot.

Man vs Zone

Public info — both teams see the scheme. In v0 both use the same proximity check; the distinction grows in v1. Switch assignment mid-possession costs 2 clicks.

Winning

Reach 21 and the game ends on that shot.
