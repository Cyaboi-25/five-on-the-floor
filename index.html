Play-by-Play

How to Play

▾

The Goal

First team to 21 wins. Build a good look, then cash the shot.

Turn Structure

Each team gets 3 action points per turn, spent across any of your 5 players however you like. Offense acts → Defense reacts → repeat. Unused points are lost — they don't carry over.

The Shoot Rule

You can only shoot as the very first action of your turn. Once you move, pass, or do anything else, you can't shoot until next turn. Use one turn to build the look; start the next turn with the shot.

The Ball

One offense piece is the ball carrier (orange dot). Only the ball carrier can shoot or pass. Pass moves the ball to a teammate — but defenders in the lane can deflect it for a steal.

Fast Pieces ⚡ + Stamina

Pieces marked ⚡ can burst 2 tiles in one action (gold-highlighted tiles) at the cost of 1 stamina. Stamina refills each possession.
